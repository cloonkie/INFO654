Who is Cloonk?

Kevin, who also goes by Cloonk, is currently attending the Fashion Institute of Technology's Bachelor's program in Advertisement and Marketing Communications, with an Associate's for Fashion Merchandising.​

Kevin is ecstatic to implement his creative yet unconventional vision into his work. He's always searching for ways to optimize workflow through automations. During his free time, he enjoys working with different mediums such as photography, jewelry designing, and tattoo work.​

Fusing his experience in business and data analytics with his creative nature, Kevin is always eager to collaborate and work on various projects.
